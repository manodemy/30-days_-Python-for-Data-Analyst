Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026

7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a full refund within 7 days of your purchase date. If you are not satisfied with the course for any reason, contact us and we will process a complete refund — no questions asked. Refund requests submitted after the initial 7-day window will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis at our sole discretion.

How to Request a Refund

  • Email support@manodemy.com with your registered email address
  • Include your Order ID or transaction reference
  • Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days

Refund Method

Refunds will be credited back to the original payment method:

  • UPI/Cards/Net Banking (Razorpay): Refunded to original account within 5–7 business days
  • Stripe (International Cards): Refunded to original card within 5–10 business days
  • PayPal: Refunded to PayPal account within 3–5 business days

After Refund

Once a refund is processed, your course access will be revoked. You may repurchase at any time.

Contact

For any refund-related queries, email support@manodemy.com
