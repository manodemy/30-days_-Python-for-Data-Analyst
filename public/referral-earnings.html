ℹ️ Referral Program Rules

How does commission calculation work?
Commission is determined by WHERE THE BUYER is located. If they pay in INR, you earn the INR rate (₹200). If they pay in USD, you earn the USD rate ($2). Cross-border conversions happen automatically at the live market exchange rate.
What is the 7-day lock period?
To prevent fraud and account for refunds, all referral commissions are locked for 7 days. Once the 7-day period expires without a refund, the earnings automatically move to your "Available Balance."
Is there an expiration on earnings?
Yes, confirmed earnings must be withdrawn within 180 days. After 180 days of inactivity, the balance is forfeited. Make sure to request your withdrawals regularly.
Are there referral caps?
Yes, to keep the program legally safe for solo operator models without requiring complex TDS/GST registrations, you can earn up to ₹10,000 INR (for Indian accounts) or $120 USD (for foreign accounts) per financial year.
