Privacy Policy

Operational Integrity Verified

At Manodemy, we are committed to safeguarding your privacy and protecting the integrity of your personal training records. This policy details how we handle information across the V2 intelligence stack and interactive cloud notebook compiler environment.

Direct Data Minimization
We only capture the absolute minimum fields required to authenticate active lessons and track progress. We do not store tracking cookies.
PCI-DSS Tokenization
All transaction processes are handled via high-security encrypted gateways (Stripe, Razorpay, PayPal). We retain zero exposure to card parameters.

01 Information We Collect

To enable the advanced browser-based compiler and progression ledger, we securely capture the following scopes:

  • Identity Metadata: Full name and email address provided during account creation or validated via single sign-on (Google OAuth).
  • Workspace Telemetry: Course completion indicators, solved daily notebook milestone scores, and timestamped activity records.
  • Payment Reference Details: Gateway-managed token structures. The raw credit card numbers or account login secrets never bypass or interact with our system servers.
  • Anonymized Localization: Temporary geolocation country-level coordinates compiled via IP lookup to ensure accurate localized pricing tiers.

02 How Your Data is Serviced

We do not operate ads, sell data ledgers, or rent telemetry logs. Your records are used exclusively to power the core educational platform:

  • To authorize and provision personalized interactive Python workspace instances.
  • To compute final milestone grades, leaderboard status, and verify verifiable Certificate of Mastery credentials.
  • To validate transactional receipts and protect against fraud.

03 Infrastructure & Cloud Isolation

Your details are hosted in highly-scalable secure server layers provisioned by Supabase (utilizing top-tier AWS virtualization servers). Row Level Security (RLS) is strictly enforced on all progress database targets, guaranteeing that your telemetry and workspace solutions remain completely isolated and invisible to outside users.

04 Third-Party Subprocessors

To deliver a premium, seamless virtual sandbox, we selectively integrate with reliable enterprise data subprocessors:

  • Supabase Inc: Authentication ledger, real-time database transactions, and cloud progress indicators.
  • Stripe / Razorpay / PayPal: Secure, encrypted billing and regional invoice compliance.
  • GeoJS Services: Secure anonymized IP parsing to automatically configure local currencies.

05 Referral Program Data

If you participate in our Referral Program, we capture minimal data to track and credit commissions, including your referral code, referred clicks, signups, purchase funnel events, and payout information (such as UPI ID or PayPal email). This data is stored securely in our encrypted Supabase database ledger and is never shared with third parties.

06 External AI Tools (Google Gemini)

The "Ask Gemini" button available inside course notebooks opens a new browser window pointing to https://gemini.google.com/ — Google's publicly available AI assistant. Manodemy does not send any user data, code, or personal information to Google when this button is clicked. The clipboard prompt is generated locally in your browser and pasted manually by you. Use of the Google Gemini service is subject entirely to Google's own Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. Manodemy is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or in partnership with Google LLC.
