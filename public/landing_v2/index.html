How is this different from traditional video courses?

+

Standard courses make you watch videos passively, which builds no real skill. Manodemy is 100% interactive. You write real code, run queries, build charts, and get automated feedback directly in your browser. No passive video bloat.

Do I need prior programming experience?

+

Not at all. The SQL, Excel, and Python modules are designed to start from absolute absolute basics and progressively build you up to advanced, industry-level data analytics skills.

How does the scoring system work?

+

We use two separate systems to guide your journey:

XP (Experience Points): Earned instantly the moment a challenge passes. It drives the competitive leaderboards, active streaks, and gamification system.

Marks: Certification-only score. Every solved concept check awards 0.649 marks. You need to earn at least 700 out of 1000 total marks to unlock your cryptographic completion certificate. Marks are tracked on your personal progress dashboard.

What is included with the AI Tutor?

+
Mano AI Tutor is available inside every notebook. If you get stuck or make a syntax error, the AI Tutor points out where the bug is, explains the error in plain English, and guides you toward solving it without just giving you the answer.

Is access instant for SQL and Excel?

+

Yes! You get instant lifetime access to all 3 tracks: 18 days of SQL, 12 days of Excel, and 30 days of Python. You can start learning and coding in any of them immediately from your dashboard.

How are certificates verified?

+

Once you achieve the required 700 marks threshold, a gold-crested, cryptographically secure certificate is generated. Each certificate has a unique ID hosted on our platform, allowing potential recruiters to instantly verify its authenticity.
