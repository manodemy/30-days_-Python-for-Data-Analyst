Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

  • Account data: Email address, name (via Google OAuth or manual signup)
  • Payment data: Processed by Razorpay/Stripe/PayPal — we never store card numbers
  • Usage data: Course progress, login timestamps
  • Location: Country code (via IP geolocation for pricing)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide course access and track your learning progress
  • To process payments securely
  • To send purchase confirmations and course updates
  • To display region-appropriate pricing

3. Payment Security

All payments are processed through PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways (Razorpay, Stripe, PayPal). We never store, process, or have access to your full card details. Payment secrets are stored exclusively in secure server-side environments.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (hosted on AWS). We use Row Level Security (RLS) policies to ensure users can only access their own data.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Authentication and database
  • Razorpay / Stripe / PayPal: Payment processing
  • GeoJS: Country detection for pricing (IP-based, anonymized)
  • Google OAuth: Optional sign-in method

6. Your Rights

  • Request a copy of your data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

7. Contact

For privacy concerns, email support@manodemy.com
